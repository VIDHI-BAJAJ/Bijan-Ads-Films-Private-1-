Lindsey Morrow

Production Manager

Lindsey has worked as a VFX editor and 1st Assistant Editor on over 30 Film and TV dramas, including “Game of Thrones”, “Skyfall”, and many British period dramas including “War and Peace” and “Vanity Fair”.

Lindsey has a bachelor’s degree in Media Theory and Production and is passionate about new software and technology that improve the story telling experience.

Gary Scullion

Snr Post Production Specialist

Gary has worked as an Assistant Editor and Editor for the past decade, working on a variety of production including the triple Emmy nominated series “The Bible”, The BBC hit comedy “Big School” and “Babylon” from Academy Award winning Director ‘Danny Boyle’.

Additionally, Gary gained some popularity creating YouTube content under the banner of ‘Sneaky Zebra’, winning awards and news notoriety from filming the Comic Con scene, as well making videos for the huge brands, including the likes of Disney, Lucasfilm, Warner, Amazon, Netflix and Universal.

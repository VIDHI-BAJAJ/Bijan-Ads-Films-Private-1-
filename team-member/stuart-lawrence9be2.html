Stuart Lawrence

Chief Technology Officer

Stuart has built and led technology teams for multiple, high-growth businesses. He’s previously been CTO at Awaze Vacation Rentals, loveholidays, and Bookatable.

Stuart built a large engineering team from scratch whilst at Expedia and has held several interim and advisory roles for both early-stage businesses and $multi-billion companies such as John Lewis Partnership, easyJet, and lastminute.com.

He is passionate about software architecture and agile development practices and holds a D.Phil in Atomic and Laser Physics from the University of Oxford.

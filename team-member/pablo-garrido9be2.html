Peter Busch

Chief Strategy Officer

With over 25 years’ experience in the entertainment industry, Peter founded and led the growth of one of the largest B2B tools companies, Faceware Technologies from 2007-2022.

Nominated in 2015 for a Scientific and Technical Academy Award, his other accolades include “The Curious Case of Benjamin Button”, “Kong vs Godzilla”, “Horizon”, “Call of Duty”, “Madden”, “FIFA”, “Halo”, and “Destiny”, and worked at Paramount Pictures, Warner Brothers feature animation, and Image Metrics.

Peter is passionate about technology and pragmatic go-to-market strategies.

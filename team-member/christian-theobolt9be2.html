Jen Hollingsworth

Chief Commercial Officer

As Chief Commercial Officer, Jen collaborates with executives and content creators to maximize scalable, positive impact, as well as overseeing the company’s exclusive partners program, cultivating partnerships that benefit from Flawless’ underlying ethos and commitment to protect creator and artist rights.

Prior to Flawless, Jen most recently served as Chief Operating Officer of the Motion Picture Group for Lionsgate where she spent 15 years. At Lionsgate, Jen closed more than $3 billion of film financing for franchises including “The Hunger Games,” “Now You See Me” and “John Wick,” as well as Oscar-winner “La La Land”.

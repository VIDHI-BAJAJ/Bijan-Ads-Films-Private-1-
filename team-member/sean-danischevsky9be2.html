Sean Danischevsky

Head of VFX

Sean is a vastly experienced Head of Visual Effects who programs and implements Machine Learning solutions. His film credits include “Batman”, “Harry Potter”, “Narnia” and “James Bond”.

Sean’s team were nominated ‘Best Visual Effects’ for “The Girl With All the Gifts”, “The Aftermath” with Keira Knightley and “Ophelia” with Daisy Ridley and Naomi Watts.
